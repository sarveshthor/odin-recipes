Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Veggies
  • Oregano
  • Chilliflakes

Steps

  1. Lay all the veggies evenly on base
  2. Spread cheese all over the base
  3. Bake the base in a oven
  4. Sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes
  5. Your pizza is ready to eat

Cheese Omelette

Description

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Ghee
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Spread ghee on a pan
  2. Break 3 eggs on the pan
  3. Sprinkle some salt and pepper on the omelette
  4. Grate some cheese and let it cook for a while
  5. Delicious omelette is ready to eat

Chicken Nuggets

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Corn Flour
  • Bread Crumps
  • Masala

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken and apply some masala and oil on it
  2. Roll it on the corn floor
  3. Put some bread crumps over it
  4. Deep fry in the Oil
  5. Crispy chicken nuggets are ready to eat
